---
title: "Monitoring & Observability"
publishedAt: "2022-09-07"
summary: "Monitoring is the process of collecting and analyzing signals to track systems and guide decisions; observability enables understanding of internal states from outputs."
---

Monitoring and observability help engineering teams track reliability and performance, identify regressions, and make informed changes. With metrics, logs, traces, and alerting, you can understand system behavior and resolve incidents faster.
